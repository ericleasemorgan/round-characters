Well, Marianne, said Elinor, as soon as he had left them, for _ one _ morning I think you have done pretty well.
You decide on his imperfections so much in the mass, replied Elinor, and so much on the strength of your own imagination, that the commendation _ I _ am able to give of him is comparatively cold and insipid.
Elinor, cried Marianne, is this fair ?
Do not boast of it, however, said Elinor, for it is injustice in both of you.
