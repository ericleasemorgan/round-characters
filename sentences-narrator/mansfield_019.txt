She knelt in spirit to her uncle, and her bosom swelled to utter, Oh, not to _ him _ !
For the present the danger was over, and Fanny ’s sick feelings subsided; but when tea was soon afterwards brought in, and Sir Thomas, getting up, said that he found that he could not be any longer in the house without just looking into his own dear room, every agitation was returning.
Not less acutely was it felt by Fanny, who had edged back her chair behind her aunt ’s end of the sofa, and, screened from notice herself, saw all that was passing before her.
Such a look of reproach at Edmund from his father she could never have expected to witness; and to feel that it was in any degree deserved was an aggravation indeed.
