The girls heard his entrance from above, and Fanny went down.
The idea of immediately seeing him, with the knowledge of what he must be suffering, brought back all her own first feelings.
He so near her, and in misery.
She was ready to sink as she entered the parlour.
He was alone, and met her instantly; and she found herself pressed to his heart with only these words, just articulate, My Fanny, my only sister; my only comfort now !
There ( holding out the paper to her ); much good may such fine relations do you.
